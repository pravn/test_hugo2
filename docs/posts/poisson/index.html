Introduction

In this post, we look at the Poisson distribution, and briefly elaborate its properties for use in Bayesian analysis (from BDA).

The Poisson distribution is characterized by a single parameter $\theta$ such that

$$ \begin{aligned} p(y|\theta) = \frac{\theta ^ y e^{-\theta}}{y!} \end{aligned}$$
